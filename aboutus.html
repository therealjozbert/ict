Our Story

>ICT is founded in 2023, in Tanzania; ICT focuses on changing how the world learns and up-skills.We are committed to equality and access to education and skills training irrespective of gender, geography, economic status or any other barriers that can so often stunt potential. So we offer a range of free courses that meet the many diverse needs of our learners.

Our Vision

At ICT, we envision a world where education is not just a privilege but a fundamental right accessible to all. Our vision is to revolutionize the educational landscape by providing free, high-quality learning resources that empower individuals to unlock their full potential, regardless of their socio-economic background.
