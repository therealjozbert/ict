Frequently Asked Questions

What is ICT?

ICTis a free online education platform that provides access to a wide range of educational resources, courses, and materials.

Is ICT really free?

Yes, ICT is completely free. We believe in making education accessible to everyone.

Do I need to create an account to access the content?

ICT offers a diverse range of courses, including academic subjects, skill-based courses, and more. Explore our catalog to discover the variety.

Can I download course materials for offline access?

Currently, offline access is not available, but you can access the materials as long as you have an internet connection. Technical Support:

I'm experiencing technical issues. What should I do?

Check your internet connection and browser settings. If the issue persists, contact our support team at support@ictknowledge.com for assistance.

Are there mobile apps available for ICT?

We are actively working on developing mobile apps for a better learning experience. Stay tuned for updates! Certificates and Recognition:

Do you provide certificates upon course completion?

Yes, we offer certificates of completion for certain courses. Check the course details for information on certificates.

Are ICT certificates recognized by employers?

While our certificates showcase your commitment to learning, recognition depends on the employer's policies. We recommend checking with them directly. Content Contribution:

Can I contribute educational content to ICT?

We appreciate your interest! Currently, content contribution is managed internally. If you have suggestions or feedback, feel free to contact us.
